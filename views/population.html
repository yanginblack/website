World's population charts

This project is using d3JS and angularJS to show an intuitive feeling of the world's population growing.
The data I use is from The World Bank. It includes population of major coutries and areas from 1960 to 2014.
I choose the top 10 coutry with largest population on selected year.
Click Play you will see the changes through years.

City Population Map

This map is showing population of cities.
Each circle represents a city, position is based on longtitude and latitude of that city,
and radius is based on population.
Surprisingly, it can give us a world map.
Data is from GeoNames extract, showing 4272 cities with population.
Idea is from Curran Kelleher, he finds this amazing way on his github to show city population as a map.
